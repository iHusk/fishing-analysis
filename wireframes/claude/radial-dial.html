Radial Speed-Dial Rationale

Paradigm: boat-glove rotary control, with length as the only hero decision.

How it works: drag the big dial or tap +/-; species, kept/released, and depth sit as thumb-reach satellites around it.

No-scroll strategy: tier-1 controls occupy the dial, lure defaults use a compact row, and auto/optional fields stay in a short bottom strip.

Outside-box angle: it treats catch logging like a physical fish finder knob, not an iOS form.

Risks: rotary precision and dense optional fields need daylight, glove, and rough-water testing.
