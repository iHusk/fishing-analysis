Concept 3 - Voice-first / conversational

Paradigm: a push-to-talk fishing log where speech fills the form and the form becomes a correction checklist.

How it works: Tyler holds the mic, says the catch once, then taps any parsed chip to fix length, species, depth, colors, bait, temp, location, or keep status before saving.

No-scroll strategy: the phone uses fixed bands: defaults, mic hero, compact chips, GPS/notes, and a bottom save rail. Optional fields are small but still visible.

Outside-box angle: the screen feels like confirming a conversation, not filling a form; it also creates a natural future home for BLE or full voice mode.

Risks: wind/noise can misparse speech, so every value remains tappable, high-contrast, and large enough for wet one-handed correction.
