Concept 4 - One-field swipe deck

Paradigm: a stack of single-purpose cards, with one oversized decision active at a time.

How it works: angler and trip defaults pre-fill the deck; tap plus/minus or swipe/tap Next to advance through kept, depth, lure, optional, save.

No-scroll strategy: each card is fixed-height, and the 12-card index compresses every required field into visible status chips.

Outside-box angle: the deck treats data entry like clearing a queue, making a future two-button BLE remote and voice confirmation natural.

Risks: hidden future cards can feel slow if defaults are wrong, and the deck index must stay tappable enough in glare.
